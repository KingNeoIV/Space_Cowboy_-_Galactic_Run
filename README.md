# 🚀 SPACE COWBOY: GALACTIC RUN  
### A Space‑Western Hybrid Arcade Shooter

**Space Cowboy: Galactic Run** is a fast‑paced hybrid action game inspired by **Galaga**, **Double Dragon**, and **Strider**.  
Fly across the galaxy as a lone outlaw, blast through enemy fleets, board massive motherships, and fight on foot with plasma 
weapons and outlaw grit. This is the wild west — just colder, darker, and infinite.

---

## 🎮 Gameplay Overview

Space Cowboy: Galactic Run blends two core gameplay modes:

### **🛸 Space Combat (Galaga‑Inspired)**
- Fast, reflex‑based shoot‑’em‑up action  
- Enemy waves, formations, and ambushes  
- Boss mothership encounters  
- Parallax planets and starfields  
- Loot drops and outlaw upgrades  

### **🤜 On‑Foot Combat (Double Dragon + Strider‑Inspired)**
- Side‑scrolling brawling and acrobatics  
- Plasma rifles, blasters, and energy blades  
- Boarding missions inside enemy motherships  
- Planet exploration with unique enemies and hazards  
- Boss captains with multi‑phase fights  

You seamlessly transition between flying, fighting, looting, and escaping as you carve your legend across the Last Frontier.

---

## 🌌 Story & Setting

You are **The Space Cowboy** — a drifting outlaw surviving in a galaxy abandoned by law and order.  
Travel between planets, raid syndicate bases, destroy rogue fleets, and take down captains for loot and reputation.

The galaxy is divided between:

- **Iron Syndicate** — ruthless criminal cartel  
- **Raider Clans** — chaotic pirate warbands  
- **Star Marshals** — broken law enforcement  
- **Remnant Fleet** — ancient war machines still fighting forgotten wars  

Every system is dangerous. Every ship is a threat. Every planet hides opportunity.
