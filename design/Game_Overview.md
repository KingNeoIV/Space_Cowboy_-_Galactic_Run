# SPACE COWBOY: GALACTIC RUN  
## Game Overview

**Space Cowboy: Galactic Run** is a hybrid arcade experience inspired by *Galaga*, *Double Dragon*, and *Strider*. It blends high‑speed space
combat with fast, stylish on‑foot action as you travel across a lawless galaxy where the wild west never died — it just traded horses for starships.

You play as a lone outlaw drifting from system to system, robbing convoys, raiding planets, boarding enemy motherships, and taking down captains
for loot, reputation, and survival. Space is vast, uncharted, and unforgiving, and every jump into a new sector brings danger, opportunity, and 
the promise of a bigger score.

The game combines classic retro shoot‑’em‑up gameplay with side‑scrolling combat sequences, creating a seamless loop of flying, fighting, looting,
and escaping. Starfields scroll beneath you, planets drift by in layered parallax, and enemy factions hunt you across the void. Every encounter is
a chance to carve your legend into the galaxy — or die trying.

---

## Core Fantasy

- You are a **space outlaw**, a gunslinger who fights in a starfighter and on foot.  
- The galaxy is the **last frontier**, full of gangs, syndicates, and hostile factions.  
- Combat is fast, chaotic, and skill‑based — in space and on the ground.  
- You raid motherships, kill captains, loot their ships, and escape before reinforcements arrive.  
- You land on planets, explore settlements, and fight with plasma rifles, blasters, and futuristic melee weapons.  
- The tone is **retro‑futuristic western**: dusty morals, fast reflexes, neon grit, and laser fire.

---

## Genre & Inspiration

**Primary Genre:** Hybrid Arcade Action  
- Space Shoot‑’Em‑Up (Galaga‑style)  
- Side‑Scrolling Combat (Double Dragon / Strider‑style)

**Primary Inspirations:**  
- *Galaga* — enemy waves, patterns, reflex‑based space combat  
- *Double Dragon* — gritty outlaw attitude, brawler energy  
- *Strider* — fast, stylish on‑foot combat and boss encounters  

The goal is to merge classic arcade simplicity with modern hybrid gameplay and a strong outlaw identity.

---

## Target Experience

Players should feel:

- Fast, reactive, and constantly in motion  
- Like a renegade carving a bloody trail across the galaxy  
- Immersed in a world of planets, starfields, motherships, and hostile factions  
- Rewarded for precision, timing, aggression, and situational awareness  
- Excited to switch between space combat and on‑foot action seamlessly  

The game is designed to be easy to pick up, hard to master, and endlessly replayable.

---

## High‑Level Features

- Multi‑layer parallax space environments  
- Fast arcade‑style space combat  
- Enemy waves, ambushes, and boss motherships  
- Boarding missions with side‑scrolling combat  
- Planet exploration with on‑foot action  
- Plasma rifles, blasters, energy blades, and outlaw weapons  
- Custom UI with sci‑fi western aesthetic  
- Ship upgrades, loot, and outlaw progression (planned)  
- Expanding lore, factions, and territories  

---

## Design Goals

1. **Capture the spirit of Galaga** while expanding it into a modern hybrid experience.  
2. **Blend western outlaw themes** into a sci‑fi setting with personality and grit.  
3. **Create seamless transitions** between space combat and on‑foot action.  
4. **Keep gameplay tight and readable**, even during chaotic encounters.  
5. **Build modular systems** that support future levels, weapons, and factions.  
6. **Establish a strong visual identity** through UI, planets, ships, and environments.  

---

## Narrative Summary

In a galaxy abandoned by law and order, the frontier has stretched into the stars. You are a space cowboy — a drifter, a thief, a survivor. You
raid ships, loot captains, land on planets, and fight your way through hostile territories. Every faction wants you dead, every system is a
battleground, and every jump forward is a gamble.

You take what you want.  
You fight who you must.  
You leave wreckage in your wake.

Space is the last frontier.  
And you aim to claim it — one ship, one planet, one legend at a time.
